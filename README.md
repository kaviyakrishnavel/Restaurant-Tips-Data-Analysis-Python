# Restaurant Tips Data Analysis(Python)

## Project Overview
This project focuses on Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of a restaurant tips dataset using Python.
The goal is to understand customer tipping behavior and identify patterns based on gender, day, time, and total bill amount.

## Dataset
- The dataset contains information about:
- Total bill amount
- Tip amount
- Gender of customer
- Day of visit
- Time (Lunch/Dinner)
- Size of the group

## Tools & Libraries Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Numpy
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook
  
## Key Analysis Performed
- Data cleaning and inspection
- Analysis of tips vs total bill
- Tip comparison by gender
- Sales and tips distribution by day and time
- Visualization of customer behavior patterns

## Key Insights
- Tips generally increase with total amount
- Dinner time shows higher average tips that lunch
- Weekend days have higher customer footfall
- Group size impacts total bill and tips

## Conclusion
This analysis helps understand customer tipping behavior and can support business decision such as staffing,pricing, and service optimization.
